Old Main

Historic Old Main housed classrooms for many years, and later served as a residence hall. Today Old Main is home to nondenominational Nevin Chapel, which hosts a variety of services and doubles as a recital hall for intimate musical concerts. Old Main also houses the offices of Franklin & Marshall's leading administrators, including the president, provost and dean.
